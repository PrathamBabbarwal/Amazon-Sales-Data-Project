# Data-Analytics-Projects
The analysis shows that Sub-Saharan Africa generates the highest revenue at ~40M, while North America (~6M) and Central America and the Caribbean (~9M) contribute significantly less, indicating growth opportunities in these regions.

The Cosmetics category is the most profitable, contributing ~14.56M in profit and around 26% of total revenue, making it the top-performing high-margin segment.

Additionally, offline sales dominate with ~58% of total revenue (~54.17M) and ~56% of total profit (~24.92M), outperforming online channels and highlighting potential to scale digital sales further.
